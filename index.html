Bin Ren (任斌)

I am a second-year Ph.D. student in the Italian National Artificial Intelligence Program co-organized by the University of Pisa, Italy, and the University of Trento, Italy. I am in the Multimedia and Human Understanding Group (MHUG) advised by Prof. Nicu Sebe and Prof. Rita Cucchiara.

My research interests lie in the intersection of computer vision, deep learning. Recently I work on privacy-preserving of Vit, and image animation with generative models in computer vision applications.

Prior to my Ph.D. studies, I received my master’s degree (2021) under the supvision of Prof. Hong Liu in computer applied technology at the School of Electronics and Computer Engineering, Peking University, China, and received my B.Eng. degree (2016) in the College of Mechanical and Electrical Engineering, Central South University, China.
