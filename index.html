Yue Song

I am a second-year Ph.D. student with Multimedia and Human Understanding Group (MHUG) at the University of Trento, advised by Nicu Sebe.

My research interests lie in the intersection of computer vision, deep learning, and numerical analysis. My most recent research activities focuses on investigating and designing robust numerical methods in computer vision applications..

Prior to my Ph.D. studies, I received the B.Sc. *cum laude* from KU Leuven, Belgium and the joint M.Sc. *summa cum laude* from the University of Trento, Itatly and KTH Royal Institute of Technology, Sweden, co-advised by Nicu Sebe and Kevin Smith.
